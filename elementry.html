Welcome!

The people make the difference at Peace With Christ Christian School. Our faculty and staff are skilled in bringing out the best in your child academically, relationally, creatively, and spiritually. We provide an environment in which your child will thrive.

And you can trust that your child will experience the same faith-based values throughout his or her school day that you instill at home. Every day, our teachers share the love of Jesus Christ and teach Biblical truth, strengthening each child’s faith and developing servant leaders.

  • • Nationally accredited through NLSA
  • • Technology integrated in all grade levels
  • • Saxon Math program for advanced learners
  • • Certified reading specialist support
  • • Departmentalized middle school structure
  • • 1-to-1 iPad program (middle school)
  • • Weekly chapel and mission outreach
  • • Before & after school care available
