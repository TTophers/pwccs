STUDENT LIFE

GROWING IN FAITH, FRIENDSHIP, AND PURPOSE

Life as an Eagle

Peace With Christ students are known, encouraged, and challenged each day. From classroom learning to chapel, service projects, field trips, and school traditions, student life is built to help children grow academically, socially, and spiritually.

Our school community gives students room to discover their gifts, build lasting friendships, and practice servant leadership in age-appropriate ways. Every student matters, and every moment is an opportunity to point one another back to Christ.

Student Opportunities

Faith Formation

Weekly chapel, classroom devotions, and mission outreach help students connect faith with daily life.

Community

Small-school relationships give students a place to belong, participate, and be supported by caring adults.

Activities

School events, service days, classroom celebrations, and enrichment activities make learning memorable.

Life at PWCCS

Athletics & Activities

Our athletic program helps students grow physically, socially, and spiritually through teamwork, leadership, and competition.

  • Team sports and physical development
  • Field Day and school-wide competitions
  • Extracurricular activities and enrichment programs

Chapel & Faith Life

Weekly chapel services include worship, scripture, and messages from staff. Students also participate in Faith Families, building relationships across grade levels through service and faith-centered activities.

Student Leadership

Student Council and Safety Patrol give students opportunities to lead, serve, and support the school community.

Technology

Students build 21st century skills through coding, digital citizenship, and hands-on projects using Chromebooks in weekly tech classes.

Lunch Program

Healthy meals are provided through Fuel 4 Fitness, offering balanced nutrition options for students each school day.

Dress Code & Spirit Wear

Our standardized dress code promotes a respectful and focused learning environment. Spirit wear is available throughout the year, allowing students to represent their school with pride.

Field Day Fun

One of the most exciting days of the year, Field Day brings students together for games, friendly competition, and unforgettable memories. It’s a celebration of community, teamwork, and school spirit.
