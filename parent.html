PARTNERING WITH FAMILIES

A School and Family Partnership

Parents are an important part of the Peace With Christ community. We value open communication, shared support, and the trust families place in our teachers and staff each day.

Whether you are checking upcoming events, connecting with teachers, volunteering, or looking for school resources, this page is a starting point for staying involved in your child's school experience.

Ways Families Stay Connected

Communication

Teachers and school staff work with families so students are supported at school and at home.

Events

School gatherings, chapel moments, classroom celebrations, and service opportunities build community.

Resources

Important school information, schedules, and next steps are easy to find through the office and school pages.

FastDirect Parent Portal

Stay connected with your child’s academic progress, announcements, and school communication through our parent portal.

V.I.P. Program

Our Very Important Parent program encourages families to get involved and serve our school community. Each family is encouraged to contribute 20 volunteer hours each year.

Volunteers help with events, classrooms, and school initiatives—making a lasting impact on students.

VIEW OPPORTUNITIES

C.A.R.E. Board & Events

Our parent-led CARE Board brings our biggest school events to life—from fundraisers to family nights and celebrations.

Spirit Wear

Show your school pride with PWCCS spirit wear! Families can order apparel like hoodies, t-shirts, and pullovers throughout the year.

It's a great way to represent our school in the community.

Chapel & Faith Life

Parents are always welcome to join us for weekly chapel. Together, we grow in faith through worship, scripture, and meaningful messages.
